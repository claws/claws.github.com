Welcome

This is my small piece of the interwebs where I can post about techno-geekery and other things I find interesting.

On the rare occasion I get some spare time at home, I enjoy tinkering around on my computer or messing around in the shed. That could involve writing some software (mostly in Python), re-wiring a Fisher and Paykel smart drive motor for use in a Savonius style wind generator or perhaps doing some genealogy research. Whatever it might be, if I think it's interesting I'll try to post about it here.
